Pac-Man

A classic from the 80s, Pac-Man is a maze arcade game developed by Namco. The objective of the game is to consume all the white dots within the maze while avoiding four colored ghosts. Power pellets can be consumed to turn ghosts into blue ghosts which Pac-Man can consume for bonus points.

Super Mario 64

Super Mario 64 - Dire Dire Docks

Super Mario 64 is a 3D platformer game developed for the Nintendo 64. It features open-world playability, degrees of freedom through all three axes in space, and relatively large areas which are composed primarily of true 3D polygons. The game is believed to be one of the main contributors to the Nintendo 64's success.

Deemo

Rapdit - Saika

Deemo is a rhythm video game developed by Rayark on the Unity Engine which was released in November 2013 on Android and iOS. The game currently has over 10 million players. The game features several different artists with story incorporated into the gameplay from the songs to world building.

The game has been released on other platformers, with versions available on the PlayStation Vita, PlayStation 4, and the Nintendo Switch.

League of Legends

K/DA - More (ft. Madison Beer, (G)I-DLE, Lexie Liu, Jaira Burns, Seraphine)

Created in 2009, League of Legends is a Multiplayer Online Battle Arena developed by Riot Games. One of the largest esports and maintaining a large following on streaming platforms, the game has over 100 million players. It is playable on Windows and macOS and the mobile version, League of Legends: Wild Rift, in beta this year.

In 2018, Riot Games collaborated with selected artists to create the virtual pop group K/DA that merged Riot's unique music with pop and K-pop influences. The group's debut song POP/STARS currently has over 400 million views. In October 2020, K/DA released their first EP, ALL OUT, with the title track MORE.

Animal Crossing

Animal Crossing is a life simulation videogame developed by Nintendo in 2001. Initially released on the Nintendo 64, it was made compatible with the GameCube. The game's village is populated by anthropomorphic animals. In an open-world environment, the game is played in real-time where players can complete different tasks and goals.
